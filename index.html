Play the card game Casino offline

This is an offline application for OS X made in Python using pygame and py2app.

Rules

This is a 2-player game where the goal is to get the most points; the first player to reach 21 points wins. There are 11 points available each round, which will be split between you and your opponent (the computer). This site has a great explanation of the rules.

How to Play

Click on the card from your hand that you want to play, type the letter "t" to take, "b" to build, or "d" to discard, then click on the card(s) on the table that you want to take/build. Press enter when you're done with your turn and wait for the computer to move!
